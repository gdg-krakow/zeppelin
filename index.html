About GDG DevFest

Conference

A day full of talks about newest technologies presented by European speakers. There will also be a soft skills set with practical tips & tricks regarding leadership, communication & design.

Workshops

3 workshops of 1,5 hour each, related to the topics introduced in presentations. That will be a great occasion to try and develop something new using up to date technologies under the supervision of experts.

Networking

During the conference you will have a lot of occasions to meet interesting people from Poland and Europe. Conference will end with an afterparty in one of Krakow’s clubs.
